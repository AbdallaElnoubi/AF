Classes

Alexandria Fencing Academy offers introductory classes to all ages. Our classes provide a great place to learn the Olympic sport of fencing.

Beginner classes start the first week of each month.

Please contact us for class availability and enrollment.

Fencing I (Beginners)

This class meets once a week for four weeks. No fencing knowledge or experience is needed. Our professional instructors will introduce the three weapons and the proper techniques of fencing skills. The class also includes fencing conditioning exercises and footwork drills.

Fencing I will help you develop strength, speed, and thinking skills, but most importantly it is FUN!!!

Fencing II

This class is for fencers who have completed Fencing I (beginner) class and are ready to learn more advanced and defense techniques.

Fencing II class schedule is available at Alexandria Fencing Academy. Please contact our academy for more information.

All fencing equipments for fencing I & II are provided.

What to wear?

T-shirt, sweat pants, and tennis shoes
