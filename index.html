Fencing for all ages

Fencing is FUN!

Fencing is a GREAT EXERCISE!

Fencing is a sport for ALL AGES.

Alexandria Fencing Academy is the prime-fencing academy in Alexandria Egypt. We provide beginning, intermediate and advanced classes in Foil, Epee and Sabre. We also have programs for competitive and recreational fencers.

Fencing is excellent for developing cardiovascular strength, muscle mass, quick reaction time, coordination, and certainly not least of all...the ability to think quickly and critically.

Fencing enables fencers to gain self-control and increase concentration and soon this self-control will begin to extend to other aspects of life.

Musketeer Program
For our youngest fencers, ages 5 to 7, we at Alexandria Fencing Academy have what we affectionately call the Musketeer program
Adult Program
Designed for recreational and competitive Adult fencers who would like to come to the open fencing sessions and/or take some individual lessons
 
Youth Program
Designed for fencers between the ages 8 to 14 who would enjoy the sport by fencing with others at Alexandria Fencing Academy
Competitive Program
Designed for competitive fencers who would like to achieve a top national ranking. This highest ranking provides an opportunity to receive a scholarship at a top U.S. university.
