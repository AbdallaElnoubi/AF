Membership


Basic Membership

Designed for fencers who enjoy the sport by fencing with others at Alexandria Fencing Academy.

Membership includes:

  • Group fencing sessions, three days a week, includes warm up, foot work, blade work, and lesson. Days and times are age dependent.
  • Discounts on camps and tournament fees.
Cost: Various payment options are available. Talk to Coach Emara for details.

Competitive Membership

Competitive Membership is for fencers who would like to achieve a top USFA ranking.

Membership includes:

  • Unlimited access to the academy during hours of operation.
  • Group fencing sessions, three days a week which includes warm up, foot work, blade work, and lesson. Days and times are age dependent.
  • Discounts on camps and tournament fees.
  • Participation in an additional intensive group training session on Mondays, 6:15pm to 7:30 pm
  • Bouting techniques.
Cost: Various payment options are available. Talk to Coach Emara for details.
